Welcome to My Website

Hello! My name is Ben Weiss, and I'm passionate about mobile app development. I created this website to share more about my journey, interests, and experiences.

I am currently a 4th-year Computer Science major at Northeastern University, where I have been honing my skills in software development. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong interest in mobile app development, which led me to pursue two internships at Vivint, a company that specializes in smart home technology.

During my internships I worked on iOS development. Both experiences were incredibly rewarding, allowing me to dive deep into the mobile development lifecycle. I found the process of building intuitive, user-friendly mobile applications fascinating and enjoyed the challenge of making each app perform seamlessly across a range of devices. These internships not only enhanced my technical skills but also taught me how to collaborate effectively in a professional development environment.

The mobile development field truly captivated my interest, and I am excited to continue in this direction. My upcoming full-time job will involve Android development, where I will have the opportunity to further expand my knowledge and contribute to creating meaningful mobile solutions. I’m thrilled to take what I’ve learned during my internships and apply it to my future career, and I look forward to continuing my journey in mobile development.
